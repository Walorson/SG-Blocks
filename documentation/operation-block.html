Operation Block

  • The Block that enables to calculate numbers and save the result to a variable.

Properites

  • Mathematical Operations (Default: 2+2): A textarea for your calculations.
  • Rounding (Default: None): Rounding mode for your result.
  • to decimal places (Default: 0): What decimal place should you round to?
  • Save to Variable (Default: var1): Variable where the final result is saved.

Use

  • To make various calculations on numerical variables.
  • To make calculatons on normal numbers.
var1 = 2+2
